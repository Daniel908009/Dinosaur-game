# example facts
Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles that have lived on Earth for about 245 million years.
The term "Dinosauria" was coined in 1842 by the English naturalist Sir Richard Owen.
Dinosaur fossils have been found on all seven continents.
All non-avian dinosaurs went extinct about 66 million years ago.
The longest dinosaur egg ever found was 2 feet long.
Almost all carnivorous dinosaurs had hollow bones to make them lighter and more agile.
The most dangerous dinosaur was the Tyrannosaurus rex
